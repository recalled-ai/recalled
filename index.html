Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 17, 2026

reCalled ("we," "our," or "us") provides intelligent call management and transcription services. We are committed to a "Privacy-First" approach, ensuring you have full control over your conversation data.

1. Permissions & Data Access

As explained in our app's onboarding, reCalled requires specific permissions to function:

  • Call Detection: To identify call start/end times and automate the transcription trigger.
  • Contact Names: To resolve phone numbers into names for your local history.
  • File Storage: To access your chosen call recording folder for processing.
  • System Overlays: To display summaries and insights immediately after a call.
  • Notifications: To alert you when background processing or cloud-enhanced transcripts are ready.

2. Hybrid AI Processing (Offline vs. Online)

We provide two distinct modes of operation to balance high-level security with advanced AI performance:

  • Offline Mode (Standard): All Speech-to-Text and AI Summarization is performed locally on your device. In this mode, audio recordings never leave your phone.
  • Online Mode (Cloud-Enhanced): To access higher-accuracy transcription and advanced multilingual summaries, users may opt-in to cloud processing. This involves a secure, encrypted upload of audio files to our protected cloud storage for temporary processing.

3. AI Technology Disclosure

reCalled utilizes advanced Artificial Intelligence to convert speech to text and generate insights:

  • Transcription: We use both on-device engines and industry-leading cloud AI partners to process audio.
  • Summarization: Advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) analyze transcripts to generate "Short Recaps" and "Key Facts."
  • Translation: We utilize on-device translation tools to provide English versions of your transcripts without requiring external data transfers when possible.

4. Cloud Security & Data Handling

When you choose to use cloud-enhanced features, we implement strict security measures:

  • Authentication: We use Firebase (Google) to verify your account and manage usage quotas.
  • Secure Uploads: Audio is uploaded via "Signed URLs"—temporary, encrypted links that ensure your data is sent directly to secure storage without being exposed to the public internet.
  • Auto-Deletion: We implement strict data retention policies where cloud-processed audio files are automatically removed after successful transcription and delivery to your device.

5. Service Tiers & Quotas

reCalled provides different tiers of service (Guest, Signed-In, and Pro). Online processing is subject to daily or monthly limits (quotas) based on your account status. Usage data is stored securely in our cloud database to ensure fair access to resources.

6. Third-Party Services

We utilize trusted infrastructure providers to power our service:

  • Google Firebase (Account management and notifications)
  • Google ML Kit (On-device intelligence)
  • Authorized AI Transcription Partners (For cloud-enhanced mode)

7. Data Deletion

All local transcripts and summaries are deleted if you uninstall the application. To request deletion of your cloud-synced account metadata, please contact us at the email below.
